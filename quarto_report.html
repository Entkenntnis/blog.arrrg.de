Gender Dynamics.

Norms regarding female leadership

The Swiss congregations of 2020 allow women to exercise responsibilities within the congregations to a greater extent than the congregations of 2009. This is what our data tell when we look at our five dichotomous variables about the possibility of leadership roles within congregations:

Variable name Variable description
WMNVLTR Women can hold all volunteer leadership positions that men can hold
WMNBRD Women can serve as full-fledged members of governing body or coordinating committee
WMNTCH Women can teach by themselves a class with adult men in it
WMNPRCH Women can preach at a main worship service
WMNLEAD Women can be head clergyperson or primary religious leader
