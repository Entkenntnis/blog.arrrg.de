# Solving Prime Climb

![heart](../assets/images/primeclimb.jpg){:width="300px" height="300px"}

Primeclimb is a math based board game with the goal of getting two pieces from start to exactly 101 before everyone else. It uses arithmetic on the numbers on two dice rolls as a form of movement. These dice are numbered from 1 - 10, and operations include addition, multiplcation subtraction and division. 

![heart](../assets/images/primeclimb2.png){:width="300px" height="300px"}

This is the entire board of which the game is played. All 8 pieces (2 for each player up to 4 players) begin at start or 0.
